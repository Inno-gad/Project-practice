Meet Our Expert Team

Our team of agricultural specialists is here to help you maximize your farm's potential. With years of experience and specialized knowledge, they can provide guidance on various aspects of farming.

Innocent Gad

Crop Management Specialist

Gad has over 15 years of experience in optimizing crop yields and implementing sustainable farming practices. He has worked with over 1000 farmers helping them to improve their yields through innovative techniques and careful resource management. His expertise in soil health and crop rotation has helped many farms increase productivity while reducing environmental impact.

Moses Mugo

Livestock Management Consultant

With a focus on animal welfare and sustainable practices, Moses helps farmers optimize their livestock operations. He specializes in breeding programs, nutrition management, and disease prevention strategies that improve animal health and farm profitability. Moses has developed innovative approaches to integrated livestock-crop systems that maximize resource efficiency.

Beres Chege

Agricultural Technology Expert

Beres specializes in integrating cutting-edge technology into farming operations to improve efficiency and productivity. With a background in both agriculture and information technology, he helps farmers implement IoT sensors, drone monitoring, automated irrigation systems, and data analytics to make informed decisions. His work has helped numerous farms transition to precision agriculture practices.
